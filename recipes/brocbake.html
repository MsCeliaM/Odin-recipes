Best Broccoli Bake


Broccoli, cauliflower, chicken and cheese bake! Yum!

This delicious and healthy dish is easy to whip up in no time and will have the whole family watering at the mounth.

Ingredients

  • One head Broccoli broken into florets
  • One head Cauliflower broken into florets
  • 500gram Chicken breast cut into blocks
  • 300 ml of Bechamel Sauce
  • 1 cup Grated Cheese
  • 1 tbls olive oil
  • 1 tps crushed garlic
  • 1/2 cup cheese flavoured crisps crushed
  • crushed onion and garlic, salt and peper to taste

Steps

  1. Pan fry chicken cubes with olive oil, onion and garlic until tender, put to side.
  2. Steam broccoli and cauliflower for approx 10min.
  3. In an oven safe dish - combine broccoli, chicken, bechamel sauce and 3/4 cups of cheese, mix through.
  4. Over the top sprinkle crushed crisps and rest of cheese.
  5. Oven bake on 180 degrees for 15 min or untill top golden brown.
