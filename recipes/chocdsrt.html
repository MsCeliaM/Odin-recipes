Chocolate Dessert


A decadent chocolate pudding dessert.

Enjoy this mouth watering chocolate pudding without any of the guilt as it's low in sugar and carbs. Serve with fresh rasberries to give it an extra layer of taste complexity.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/3 cup low-bard sweetner

Steps

  1. Mix whipping cream and sweetener on medium low speed until it thickens up in a large mixing bowl with an electric mixer.
  2. Add cacao powder and beat until stiff peaks form.
  3. Devide into individual containers to serve (think rammikins or whiskey tumblers) and decorate as desired.
  4. Chill in refrigerator for about 10 minutes and serve.
